About our Company

Creatively Cannabis was founded with a vision of fun, alternative events for cannabis enthusiasts.

Owner and lead artist Keila Castillo had prior experience creating dynamic "paint and sip" events while living on the East Coast. After moving to Denver in 2016, she envisioned an alternative option. Marijuana tends to increase an individual's creativity and responsiveness to all things artistic so, naturally, the idea to pair painting and cannibis arose.

Passionate about legislation and often changing marijuana reform, Keila wanted her business to help people to stay informed and proactive. Thus, the Highdeas Pot-cast was created! The Pot-cast features artists, musicians, activists, and entrepreneurs from the East and West Coast.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is cannabis included or can we purchase it at the event?

No, cannabis is not included, and Creatively Cannabis does not sell it directly.

However, cannabis is available for purchase next door at 1136 Yuma or at other recommended dispensaries.

Can I smoke and/or use an open flame?

No. Due to the Clean Air Act, all consumption must be non-combustible. This includes bowls, joints, blunts, bongs or other ways that use an open flame, such as dab rigs heated with torches.

Permitted forms of consumption include vape pens, flower vape devices, e-nails, and edibles. Other ways to ingest cannibis may be allowed; please contact us in advance if you have any questions.

The Coffee Joint has e-nails for dabbing available for purchase. We also have several flower vape devices that can be used during the event.

How much do the events cost?

Please visit our eventbrite page for presale ticket pricing. Day-of tickets are $55, cash only.

How old must I be to attend?

You must be 21+ to attend the event and must have a valid ID. IDs will be checked upon entrance.

What does my ticket cost include?

Tickets include a 16 x 20 inch canvas to paint on and keep, as well as paint, brushes, aprons, easels, and instruction for the duration of the event.

Snacks and beverages can be purchased at The Coffee Joint.

When should I arrive?

Participants are encouraged to arrive 30 minutes prior to the event. This allows time to consume cannabis and socialize prior to the event starting.

Events will start no later than 10 minutes after the posted start time.

Where are the events located?

For now, all of our public events are hosted at The Coffee Joint at 1130 Yuma St in Denver, CO.

Do you do private events?

Yes! Please contact us to request more information about booking your private or corporate event.

What clothing is appropriate?

Since we are painting with acrylics, it is recommended that participants wear "play" clothes, or anything they don't mind getting paint on.

Aprons are provided to minimize any potential messes.

What can I expect at the event?

Please see our attendance section for more info.

How can I cancel my reservation?

Reservation cancellations must be made 24 hours prior to the start of the event to guarantee transferable credit.

Cancellations received 24 hours or less from the event start time will be issued credit on a case-by-case basis.
